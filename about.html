I am an educator and designer specializing in lighting, scenery, and projections. Originally from Chicago, I received my bachelors in design and directing from Illinois State University.

I spent many years based in New York City and travelling the world designing every possible type of production you can imagine including theater, opera, dance, concerts, houses of worship, fashion shows, retail stores, and what I am pretty sure was a cult meeting-- ask me about it, it is a weird but funny story.

After a long time on the east coast, I got back to my midwest roots and am currently in my final year as an MFA candidate at Indiana University-Bloomington with a focus on design pedagogy.
